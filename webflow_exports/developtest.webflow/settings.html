Receive Daily Jokket Email?

The daily jokket provides a slice of your Jokket life in one place. Includes your daily score update, and a recap of notifications relating to hiring, working, or management (depending on how you use Jokket)

Receive hiring / management notifications as they occur?

These updates include notifications of bids that are placed on posted jobs, and any time-sensitive updates (regarding bidding, milesontes, etc.).

Receive working notifications as they occur?

Notificaitons include updates on your bid status and suggestions on making your bid more attractive (based on other bidder behavior).
